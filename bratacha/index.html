Bratacha

Bratacha is a fun flag quiz game as Gaeilge! Presently there are 195 flags oganized into six categories.

To play the game, first select a level (Whole world, Africa, America, Asia, Europe, Oceania) by clicking on the orange button before pressing play. Every correctly answered flag earns a point. At the end of a level, the player's score is displayed.

Presently the game is in the early stage of development, with the version displayed here a simple proof of concept. Here are some quick points on what I'd like to improve:

  • Additional game mode where the player must spell the name of the country.
  • Boosters which show the answer or remove two incorrect flags etc.
  • Another difficulty setting in which similar flags are displayed (i.e. for Ireland, flags of Italy, Mexico and Ivory Coast could be display along with Ireland's flag).
  • Social options: challenge your friends!
  • Bonus Levels: Flags of Ireland.
  • Additional languages: English, German, Polish and Russian.
  • User Interface Update.

Thank you for playing Bratacha. Please submit your feedback on the right-hand side of this page, it'll help make the game even better!

NOTE: This is a simulation of a mobile app, thus it takes a little longer to load than the finished game will. Also some flag graphics aren't rendered perfectly in this simulation.
